— 06 / Questions

Frequently asked
questions.

Is the Loomix Resume Maker free?

Yes — it is completely free. There is no sign-up, no watermark on your resume, and no hidden charges. You can build and download as many resumes as you like.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. Open the studio, build your resume, and download it instantly. We never ask for an email or password.

Is my data private and secure?

Completely. The entire resume builder runs inside your browser. Your personal details and resume content never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Can I download my resume as a PDF?

Yes. With one click you get a clean, print-ready PDF of your resume — with all colours and layouts preserved — ready to send to recruiters or upload to job sites.

How many resume templates can I choose from?

There are 10 professionally designed, recruiter-tested templates — from clean minimal layouts to bold modern designs — and you can switch between them anytime without losing your content.

Will my resume work with ATS systems?

Yes. Every template uses a clean, structured layout with standard section headings that applicant tracking systems (ATS) can read reliably.
